# Velura Services Email Signatures

These templates are designed for Google Workspace/Gmail signatures.

## How To Use

1. Open `docs/email-signatures/velura-signatures.html` in a browser.
2. Highlight the signature you want.
3. Copy it.
4. In Gmail, go to `Settings > See all settings > General > Signature`.
5. Create or edit the signature and paste it in.
6. Replace any placeholder text such as phone numbers, partner name, and employee details.
7. Save changes at the bottom of Gmail settings.

## Business Partner Signature

Use the same owner-level layout for the business partner.

Replace:

- `[Partner Name]`
- `[partner@veluraservices.com]`
- `[partner phone number]`

## Employee Signature

Use the simpler employee layout for cleaners and team members.

Replace:

- `[Employee Name]`
- `[Employee Role]`
- `[employee@veluraservices.com]`
- `[employee or office phone number]`

## Brand Notes

- Sender name should be `Velura Services`.
- Keep the logo small so replies stay tidy.
- Use the shared booking inbox for customer booking conversations unless the employee needs a direct address.
- Avoid long quotes, images, or large banners in signatures because they make customer email threads harder to read.
